Artist Statement

I draw playful moments, exaggerated emotions, and the imagination that quietly slips into everyday life.

The work is rooted in hand-drawn texture, blending watercolor, colored pencil, markers, acrylic paint, and digital finishing for book illustration, brand visuals, event posters, and personal commissions.

作品以手绘质感为核心，结合水彩、彩铅、马克笔、丙烯颜料和数字整理，适合图书插画、品牌视觉、活动海报和个人委托。

Process

Creative Process

Clear steps from idea to final artwork, with room for mood and discovery.

  1. Brief 沟通

    We define the theme, usage, format, timeline, and visual direction.

  2. Sketch 草图

    Composition, character relationships, and the story rhythm are shaped first.

  3. Color 上色

    Color, texture, and details are refined to build the final mood.

  4. Delivery 交付

    Final files are prepared for print, web, or social publishing.
