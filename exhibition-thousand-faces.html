A Thousand Faces Creative Exhibition

Acrylic 丙烯 Watercolor 水彩 Handmade 手作 Animation 动画 Exhibition 展览 Honors 荣誉

In 2017, Mantou Art Studio and its students presented the A Thousand Faces Creative Exhibition at Xiamen Art Museum in Xiamen, China.
