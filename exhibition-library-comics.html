Xiamen Library Original Comics Exhibition: Civilized Reading

Acrylic 丙烯 Watercolor 水彩 Handmade 手作 Animation 动画 Exhibition 展览 Honors 荣誉

Created for Xiamen Library, "Civilized Reading" is an original comics project by Mantou Art Studio. The work has been shown in multiple exhibitions, presented inside Xiamen subway carriages as a public reading campaign, and extended into bookmark designs.

为厦门市图书馆创作“文明阅读”原创漫画，进行多次展览，并将漫画放进厦门地铁车厢里宣传展示，还制作了书签。
